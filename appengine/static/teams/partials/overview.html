Welcome to ROSEbotics Teams!

Teams is a place where you can create study groups and monitor others progress within the courses on the website.

In order to see if you've been invited to any teams, click on the invites tab. Here you can see what teams you've been invited to join, then give authorization to allow your progress to be reported.

To see team members' progress, go to the teams page. If you'd like to create a team, you can manage your own teams from the manage page.

Happy learning!
