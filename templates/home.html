Smart Product Design, Software Engineering, and Entrepreneurial Opportunity

ROSEbotics is a freely available, online series of courses, an annual robotics competition, and a platform to inspire innovation and entrepreneurial opportunity. Courses are based on content developed by faculty at Rose-Hulman with Rose-Hulman alumni as the target audience.

Courses

Visit our series of courses to see what ROSEbotics offers. Currently, we have a complete course in Android development, one in Web development with new content being added in late 2014, and a partial course in iOS development.

Competition

There is an annual competition planned to take place at Rose-Hulman Homecoming beginning the Homecoming 2015. The competition will have two components, a robotics challenge and a "Shark Tank" style product pitch competition, where students are encouraged to think of products they could build based on the skills they've gained.

View details »

Platform

Both the courses and the competition make HEAVY use of the ROSEbotics platform. The platform will update every few years, but the current platform uses a Bluno Nano, Rover 5 tank base, and Explorer PCB kit. The Rosebotics platform will be available beginning the summer of 2015 on the course material launch date.
